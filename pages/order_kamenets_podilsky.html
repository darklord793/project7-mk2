Kamenets Podilsky

We invite you to know the beauty of the picturesque Podillya, caves and irresistible fortresses!

Day 1: Departure from Lviv (08:00) - Kamyanets-Podilsky (excursion along the fortress, lunch, city tour, settlement to the hotel)

Day 2: Kamenets-Podilskyi (breakfast) - Khotyn (excursion along the fortress) - Krivecha (lunch) - Bilche-Zolota (overview of Verteba cave) - Lviv (return at 22:30).

Price - 950 UAH

The price includes: travel to the comfortable Euro-class bus, guide-guided tour guide, excursion service in museums, accommodation, travel insurance.

Extras are paid: entrance tickets to museums and food.

Entrance fee:

- Verteba cave (general, pension, student - 20 UAH, students - 15 UAH );

- Khotyn fortress (adults - 50 UAH, students and students - 25 UAH);

- Kamenetz fortress (adults 40 UAH, students and students - 20 UAH).

Accommodation: hotel "7 days" in Kamyanets-Podilsky. Comfortable double rooms with amenities in the room.

Kamenets Podilsky

Kamyanets'-Podil's'kyi is a city of regional subordination in Ukraine, the center of Kamianets-Podilskyi district of the Khmelnytsky region, from March 22, 1919 to November 1920 - the capital of the UPR;

Carpathian tram

Beneficial narrow-gauge railway (modern advertising name - Carpathian tram) - a chain of mountain narrow-gauge railways (width of the track - 750 mm) in the Ukrainian Carpathian Mountains.

Dovbush rocks

Dovbush cliffs are a rock and cave complex, a unique monument of history and nature. Located 3 km from the village of Bubnyshcha, in the tract Bubnishche, on the territory of the Polyanytskyi Regional Park.

m. Hoverla

Hoverla - the highest peak of the Ukrainian Carpathians and the highest point of Ukraine, the height of 2061 m. Located in the mountain range of Chornohora, 17 kilometers from the border with Romania.
